Animation instructions

How to operate the animation?

You simply press the buttons "back" or "next" that are placed in Menu area.

How to operate the logical circuit?

By switching logical state, logical 1 or logical 0. Selector is placed

at the top of the Menu.

What if I do not know any objects?

Try to look into legend, you scrollbar to move down and maybe you

will find the solution.

Where can I get more information about CMOS?

In the Menu area, pressing the buttons Logical circuits or CMOS.

What if I can not see the scheme in details

In some parts of the animation is possibility to use Zoom, in the Menu area

Is possibility to listen any audio records?

Yes, animation principle of working CMOS invertor, has this option, in Menu area

There is possibility to pause audio record and then continue listening.

I can not see full animation, some object has missing parts

In this case, try to decrease image in the browser via zoom
